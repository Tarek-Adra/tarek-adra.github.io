About the challenge

HALF OR FULL TARGET

Challenge yourself to complete the push-up target. Aim for half or the full push-up target.

Push-ups not for you? Alternatives like sit-ups, squats or tailored exercises are all welcome.

Sign up solo or get your crew together - invite your mates, work, school, gym or sports club.
