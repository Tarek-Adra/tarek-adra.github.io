Join our team of inspirational peak performers

We’re on a mission at Peak to help students take control of their studies and achieve their academic goals! Our unique philosophy, principles, pro-active learning system, comprehensive resources and strong team values enables students in Years 10-12 to achieve their academic goals and prepares them for a life of achievement beyond school.

Our roles we offer

We’re looking for truly gifted people to join our passionate team and help students achieve success.

Our Roles

Passionate Teachers

Peak teachers inspire, lead and facilitate the delivery of fun and engaging theory lessons. We are constantly looking for talented WACE Maths, Physics and Chemistry teachers to join our team.

Supportive Tutors

Peak tutors guide and mentor students through one to one tutorials (workshops). We are constantly looking for talented WACE Maths, Physics and Chemistry tutors to join our team.

Inspirational Coaches

Peak coaches help students take control of their studies. We are constantly looking for inspirational academic coaches to join our team.

Instructional Designers

Peak instructional designers plan, strategise and build high-quality Peak Education course materials. We are constantly looking for talented WACE Maths, Physics and Chemistry instructional designers to join our team.
